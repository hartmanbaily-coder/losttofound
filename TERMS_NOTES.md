# Terms Notes

These notes are product and implementation guidance, not final legal terms.

The public `/terms` page contains a records-specific draft for counsel review before production launch.

## Not Legal Advice

Lost to Found Records helps users organize records. It does not provide legal advice, predict court outcomes, or replace consultation with a qualified attorney.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the accuracy, completeness, and appropriateness of records they enter or upload.

## No Guarantee of Court Admissibility

The app does not guarantee that any record, note, evidence item, export, PDF, CSV, or summary will be accepted or relied on by a court, agency, attorney, mediator, or other third party.

## Documentation Only

The child support module is for documentation only. The app does not process payments, collect bank login credentials, scrape bank accounts, or store full bank account/card/debit-card numbers or Social Security numbers.

## No Emergency or Safety Response

The app is not an emergency service, crisis service, law enforcement tool, or safety-response provider. Users should contact local emergency or professional services when appropriate.

## Attorney Consultation

Users should consult a qualified attorney about their situation, records, evidence handling, and any court or agency filing requirements.
